ABOUT ME

A hardware engineer designs, tests, and oversees the production of electronic hardware components and systems. They specialize in circuitry, microelectronics, and embedded systems, ensuring optimal performance and functionality in various devices, from computers and smartphones to medical equipment and automotive systems.

Drone technology encompasses unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) equipped with sensors, cameras, and navigation systems. Drones are utilized across industries for aerial surveillance, mapping, delivery, and entertainment. Advancements include autonomous flight capabilities, extended battery life, and obstacle avoidance, driving innovation in fields such as agriculture, filmmaking, and emergency response.

A VLSI engineer specializes in designing and implementing Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) circuits and systems. They utilize advanced semiconductor technologies to create integrated circuits with millions of transistors, optimizing performance, power consumption, and chip area. Their expertise drives innovation in electronics, spanning from microprocessors to consumer electronics.
